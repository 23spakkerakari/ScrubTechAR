MASTECTOMY
==========
Source: Sklar Surgical Instruments — Standard Mastectomy Set (SKU 98-1007);
        Surtex Instruments Mastectomy Set; StatPearls / NCBI Bookshelf.

Overview:
  Surgical removal of one or both breasts, performed for breast cancer treatment
  or prophylaxis. Includes simple mastectomy, modified radical mastectomy (MRM),
  and skin/nipple-sparing variants. Typical OR time: 1–3 hours.
