HIP REPLACEMENT (TOTAL HIP ARTHROPLASTY)
=========================================
Source: Ameri Surgical Instruments — Basic Hip Replacement Instrument Set, 72 Pieces;
        NurseKey "Total Hip Replacement" surgical technology guide;
        Surgical Holdings Hip Instrument Set.

Overview:
  Removal of the damaged femoral head and acetabulum cartilage/bone, replaced
  with metal, ceramic, or polyethylene prosthetic components. Performed via
  posterior, lateral, or anterior approach. Typical OR time: 1.5–3 hours.
