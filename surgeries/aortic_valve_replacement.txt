AORTIC VALVE REPLACEMENT (AVR)
==============================
Source: Association of Surgical Technologists (AST), "Aortic Valve Replacements,"
        The Surgical Technologist, January 2019 (Article #421); and
        Ameri Surgical Instruments 252-Piece Open Heart Surgical Instrument Set.

Overview:
  Open-chest cardiac surgery requiring cardiopulmonary bypass in which the
  diseased aortic valve is excised and replaced with a tissue or mechanical
  prosthesis. Median sternotomy is performed; the heart is arrested using
  cardioplegia solution. Typical OR time: 3–5 hours.
